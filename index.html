About Us

Meta Audit stands as a pioneering force in the dynamic realm of cryptocurrencies, boasting a revolutionary approach to security and transparency. As an avant-garde AI-powered platform, Meta Audit specializes in comprehensive smart contract auditing and rugpull detection, redefining the way investors and developers engage with blockchain projects. With its utility going live prior to its official launch, Meta Audit has managed to introduce a new era of confidence in the crypto space. Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, Meta Audit's AI-driven contracts auditor scans and evaluates smart contracts with unparalleled precision, identifying vulnerabilities, security gaps, and potential risks. This empowers developers with crucial insights to fine-tune their contracts and ensures that investors can engage in projects with heightened assurance. The platform's rugpull checker further cements its significance by scrutinizing transaction patterns and market data, providing a vital layer of protection against fraudulent schemes.

 

Built with the latest AI technology, the Meta Audit Dapp is designed to generate accurate and error-free audits of smart contracts. The Dapp is already live and available for use, providing users with a seamless and intuitive experience.
